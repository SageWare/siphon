FAQs

I'm a graphic artist, how can I get my work used on CDs?

Artists provide their own album artwork. Please contact our artists directly.

I have a store, who can I contact to set up carrying the releases you distribute?

Please contact us with "Attn: Distribution" in the Department field.

How can I book one of your artists?

We do not act as a booking agency for our artists. But, if you contact us with "Attn: Booking" in the Department field and describe your needs, we will be sure to help get you in contact with the necessary agency.

How can I get review copies of SIPHON RECORDS music and/or artist contacts for my magazine/media outlet?

Please contact us with "Attn: Licensing" in the Department field, and include a link to a .pdf copy of your publication. Be sure to tell us about your publication along with circulation information and official website and we will get back to you.

I want to license songs from a SIPHON RECORDS artist, who do I contact?

Please fill out the Licensing Request Form. Any questions not listed in the licensing faqs can be sent via the contact form with "Attn: Licencing" in the Department field.

Do you accept artists who are outside the United States, already with another label, or are currently registered with a different PRO than ASCAP?

Absolutely. Your unsolicited demo, or EPK, can be submitted through our demo submission form.
